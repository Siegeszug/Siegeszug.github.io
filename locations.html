1 Regions

Following The War in the West, those cities that contributed to the war effort (which were mostly on the western side of the continent) began to blame each other for the catastrophic outcome, causing them to split into The Northern Concord and The Southern Coalition. Those who did not participate in the war on the eastern side of the continent remained mostly unaffiliated with of one another and became known as The Eastern Entente.
