1 Fey in Talmiar

As far as recorded history goes, The Fey predate human presence on the continent of Talmiar; and it is theorized that widespread Fey presence on the continent grew with the maturity of the World Tree.

All Fey in Talmiar, be they Fairy, Dryad, or otherwise, are members of at least one Fey court. Courts behave much like extended families, and for many Fey have little impact on their day-to-day lives. Some high ranking members of some of the courts might have seaonal responsibilities. While there are four major courts, there are also many personal courts (also called Royal courts) for certain high-ranking fey.

2 The Major Courts

2.1 The Court of Cold

Cold Fey enjoy Ice, and snow, and the bite of clean, cold air, and generally dislike warm air, strong smells, or other sensations associated with summer and spring, tend toward sarcastic and/or dark humor, or else grim humorlessness, enjoy the emotional catharthis of tragic plays and music, visual art which draws the viewer in and elicits strong, abstract, emotional response, etc. Their ballads are less likely to end happily, and a WInter Fey can give an artist no greater gift than to react to their art with an open, unabashed display of emotion, especially tears.

2.2 The Court of Warm

These fey tend to enjoy the sun, growing things, lively music, optimistic art and stories, and the thrill of the hunt and/or battle. A Summer Warm Fey wants music, and laughter, and air thick with the smells and sounds of growing things.
