The World Tree

Back

1 Overview

The World Tree is an entity which is completely central to the Continent of Talmiar and The World. It is responsible for and integral to many economic, political, and religious decisions made by people in the region. The location of settlements, the bounty of harvest, and the locations of wild creatures are just a few things determined by The Tree's influence.

2 Location

The World Tree is located at the center of the continent, and at it's base is the Sovereign City of The World Tree. The city serves as a hub for many different things, but it is especially noteworthy as a city of diplomatic embassy and as the Holy City for The World Tree Faith. People are widely aware of the Sovereign City and devotees to the World Tree Faith often make at least one pilgrimage in their life.

The World Tree can be seen from most places on Talmiar, and it's height crests most mountain ranges that would otherwise obstruct it.

3 Roots

The World Tree's roots spread far and wide across the continent, and The Tree's roots grant great fertility to the surrounding land, and ward away many extra-planar beings, especially undead, demons, and devils. Where these roots crest above the ground, the densest of forests grow, which are often inhabited by fey-folk. Where the roots run below the ground tend to make good locations for settlements and cities.

The roots extend in all directions around the world tree, but they extend furthest to the north, west, and south. The eastward roots do not extend very far, and thus the eastward land is less contested. Past The Silver Tides, only the city of Branberry is reached (via the Ignobleford root).

While there have been efforts to completely map the roots, these endeavors are normally stonewalled by areas where the roots go too far beneath the ground to have an effect on the surface.

4 Seasons

The seasons in Talmiar occour spread radially from The World Tree, following the usuall Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter cycle, with a new year being marked by the usual first signs of spring in the Sovereign City. A season usually lasts 90 days, and once a season starts in the Sovereign City, it usually takes about 15 days for the rest of the continent to experience it.

5 Origin

Some speculate that The World Tree has been around since the creation of the world, while others suggest that it appeared after some cataclysmic event. Some even posit that is was simply a botanical experiment that was a bit too successful. The true origin of The World Tree is completely unknown.
