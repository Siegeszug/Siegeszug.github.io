1 The Major Races

While all of the major races are present in Talmiar, there are many differences in how they arrived there.

1.1 The Native Races

Humans, Halflings, and Gnomes are native to the continent, and have been living there as equals since the beginning of the current age.

1.2 The Immigrant Races

The Elves and Dwarfs began immigrating from the western continent a few years after the invading force of Talmiar conceded and requested peace. Due to their impressive craftsmanship, the Elves and Dwarfs are welcomed into cities with open arms. The supperior craftsmanship of the two races led to the creation of The School of Magic Craftsmanship, which encouraged the proliferation of newly-made magic items across Talmiar.

Though there is some remaining prejudice from the native populations against the Elves and Dwarfs, it is mostly from the elderly population who can still remember the war with the western continent.

Although Elves and Dwarfs are known to quarrel on a personal level quite often, racial tensions between them are actually quite low, having immigrated from the same home continent.

Half-Elfs and Half-Dwarfs are common (although Half-Dwarfs are funtionally indestinguishable from Humans and Dwarfs).

2 The Minor Races

There are many minor races in Talmiar, but most inhabit only very small regions of land, or are very few in number. Among the more common are Lizardfolk, Tiefling (who tend to disguise themselves), and Half-Orcs.
